High Protein Chicken

Protein: 35g | Calories: 400kcal | Preparation time: 45 min

Delicious and healthy meal to help you stay in shape and build muscle.

Preparation Steps:

  1. Cut the chicken into pieces and marinate with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  2. Fry it in a pan until golden brown.
  3. Add vegetables and simmer on low heat for 15 minutes.
  4. Serve with a side of your choice.
